About Eddie D's

Eddie D's stands out as a quintessential New York pizzeria, capturing the essence of traditional pizza-making with a charm that resonates with locals and visitors alike. With over forty years of experience, Eddie has become a beloved figure in the Valley Stream community, dedicated to crafting exceptional pies that are as visually appealing as they are delicious. Each pizza features a thin, crispy crust paired with generous toppings, high-quality cheese, and a unique sauce blend.
